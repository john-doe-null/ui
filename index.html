Strawberry Milkshake Decoden Phone Case Tutorial 🍓✨

Learn how to create this adorable, squishy-looking strawberry milkshake themed phone case! I'll walk you through mixing the perfect pastel pink shade of cream glue and arranging resin charms for that perfectly balanced, 'poured' look. Perfect for beginners and advanced crafters alike!

#Decoden #Kawaii #DIYTutorial

Production Steps

1

Prep the Case & Glue

Rough up the back of your clear phone case lightly with sandpaper for better adhesion. Mix your clear cream glue with 2 drops of pastel pink pigment.

2

Piping the Cream

Using a star piping tip, carefully pipe the pink cream glue around the edges first, then fill in the center using soft, circular motions to create volume.
